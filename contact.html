Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order in real-time through the Foodigo app or website. Click on "Your Orders" and select the order you want to track.

What is your refund policy?

We offer full refunds for cancelled orders. For issues with delivered orders, contact our support team within 48 hours.

How do I become a restaurant partner?

Visit our restaurant partnership page or email partners@foodigo.com. Our team will guide you through the onboarding process.

Is Foodigo available in my city?

Foodigo operates in 50+ cities across India. Enter your location on our website to check availability.

How can I apply for a job at Foodigo?

Visit our careers page at careers.foodigo.com to explore current openings and submit your application.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, digital wallets, net banking, and UPI payments. Cash on delivery is also available in select areas.

Need Immediate Help?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you
